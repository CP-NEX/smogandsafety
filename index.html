Who needs a smog inspection in California?

▾

Most California vehicles need a smog inspection during registration renewal or when entering the state. Some newer models and special vehicle classes may be exempt.

When is a brake and lamp inspection needed?

▾

Brake and lamp inspections are commonly needed for revived salvage title work and may also come up during DMV or CHP follow-up situations.

What are common signs of wheel alignment problems?

▾

Pulling to one side, uneven tire wear, an off-center steering wheel, and poor handling are common reasons to schedule an alignment check.

What are signs a vehicle needs brake repair?

▾

Squeaking, grinding, vibration, a soft brake pedal, longer stopping distance, or a brake warning light are all reasons to have the vehicle checked.

Does the shop handle inspection-related repairs?

▾

Yes. Smog and Safety Inspection presents a broad service mix that includes smog checks, brake and lamp inspections, brake repair, alignment, maintenance, VIN verification, and inspection support.
